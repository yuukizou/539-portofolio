EAT PLAY LAUGH

Your Mental Wellbeing Daily Tool

Type

Class Project Interaction Design

Duration

3 months

Role

User Experience Designer

Project Brief

According to the recent annual report from Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) at University of Michigan, the number of requests from students seeking mental support has increased by 25% during the past years. Specifically, the two peak periods where high volume of requests received are the third week of September and one in second week of January, where students begin their school life after a summer or winter break. Reportedly, the increase in service requests has brought uprising challenges to the CAPS service due to a shortage of resources and the “ripple effect” along the semester. The top issues assessed by the institution are Anxiety, Depression, Relationships, Academics, and Adjustments. The result is also verified through 7 one-on-one interviews conducted during the past two weeks.
