SMART SHOES

Design Smart, Fit Well

Type

Vision Design

Duration

3 months

Role

User Experience Designer, User Assistance Developer

Project Brief

Fashion trends can be changed at a pace beyond your imagination. This dizzy speed calls for a much shorter design and production cycle of footwear. To grasp the market insights is undoubtedly valuable to design directors like John as he needs to think about how to incorporate these elements into the design of new products right in time. To ravel the secrets behind the market changes, SAP and Aimiqi, the shoe manufacturing company, set up a partnership to make the design process simpler, faster, but smarter.

Concept

Agility and speed matters more than ever in product innovation and development in digital business.Â Companies are seeking new ways to design and develop smart and connected products. The design gets the analytical insight based on machine learning results from the market and sends the result to SAP Connected Products, a cloud platform that seamlessly integrates systems engineering with virtual collaboration, orchestrates the digital twin through tight integration with S/4HANA digital core, which enables customers to design and develop smarter products in a faster manner.

Feature: Fashion Trend Tracker
